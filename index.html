About Child Dignity International

Child Dignity International is a registered indigenous NGO in Uganda, operating both in the refugee context and development. Our areas of operation encompass all regions in Uganda to support children's rights and welfare.

VISION

Child Dignity International vision looks at upholding the Child’s Dignity through creating a fully developed child.

MISSION

Child Dignity international reaches out to the vulnerable children in the world with the love, passion, and care so as to uphold a sustained life through supporting them physically, emotionally, socially and spiritually.

GOALS

Advocate for children's rights.
Provide education support through sponsorship, family engagement, and infrastructure development.
Support the holistic
well-being of vulnerable children.
Create safe and inclusive environments for vulnerable children, including those with special needs.

TARGET GROUP

For Selection Criteria, in its sponsorship and street children’s work respectively, the interpretation of Child Dignity International uses for a vulnerable child includes:
A child aged 3 to 17 years, and youth upto the age of 35 years who is at risk of dropping out of Early Childhood Education, primary and secondary schools
